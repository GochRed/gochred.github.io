About Cemar 01

Single-Point Precision. Global Scale.

Cemar International is a single-point stockist and dealer of armoring materials and ballistic protection solutions, headquartered at National Industries Park, Dubai, UAE. We design, machine, and supply components to the world's most demanding defence programmes.

Operating exclusively B2B, we serve armored vehicle manufacturers, defence contractors, military procurement divisions, law enforcement agencies, and cash-in-transit fleet builders across seven global regions.
