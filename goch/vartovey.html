Lviv, Ukraine

Categories

The Brief

Creating an authoritative brand mark that honors historical symbolism while functioning effectively across rugged physical gear and digital e-commerce environments.

Vartovey required a strong, heritage-driven visual identity paired with a modern, tactical aesthetic. The challenge was bridging historical weight with a sharp, multidisciplinary design approach suitable for both physical and digital spaces.

The Solution

A sharp, utilitarian shield emblem featuring the Ruthenian lion, executed in a high-contrast gunmetal and icy blue palette, paired with assertive, custom typography.

Hardware application showcasing the sharp curves and industrial strength of the mark.

Tactical apparel integration of the modernized Ruthenian lion shield.

Physical e-commerce packaging utilizing the high-contrast gunmetal and blue color system.

Digital storefront translation, emphasizing the brand's modern online e-commerce presence.

Social media ecosystem and digital content templates.

Large-scale environmental application for physical retail spaces.
